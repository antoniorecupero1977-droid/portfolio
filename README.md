# Quality of Life — Italian Provinces
- **Description**: Ranking of Italian provinces based on a quality of life index.
- **Dataset**: [ISTAT BES](https://www.istat.it/en/well-being-and-sustainability) / [Il Sole 24 Ore Quality of Life Ranking](https://lab24.ilsole24ore.com/qualita-della-vita/)
- **Technologies**: `pandas`, `matplotlib`
- **Output**: Top 10 provinces for quality of life.
